CS180 Project 0

Becoming Friends with Your Camera

Part 1: Selfie – The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

I took two portraits: one close-up (distorted), and one from further back with zoom (more natural proportions). This experiment shows how perspective affects facial appearance.

Part 2: Architectural Perspective Compression

I photographed the same street scene twice: first zoomed in from far away, and then from closer without zoom. The zoomed-in version looks "flattened" compared to the wide-angle one.

Part 3: The Dolly Zoom

I recreated the famous "Vertigo effect" by moving the camera backwards while zooming in, keeping the subject roughly the same size. The background perspective shifts dramatically!
