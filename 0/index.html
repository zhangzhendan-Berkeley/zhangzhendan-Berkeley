CS180 Project 0

Becoming Friends with Your Camera

Part 1: Selfie – Wrong Way vs. Right Way

I compared two portraits: one close-up (distorted) and another from further away with zoom (better proportions). This shows how perspective affects facial appearance.

Part 2: Architectural Perspective Compression

I repeated the experiment with architecture. The zoomed-in shot from afar appears "flattened", while the close-up wide-angle shot looks deeper.

Part 3: The Dolly Zoom

I recreated the famous "Vertigo shot" by moving backwards while zooming in, keeping the subject the same size. The background changes dramatically!
